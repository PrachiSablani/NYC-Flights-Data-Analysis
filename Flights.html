The flights dataset is present in the nycflights13 package in the form of the data frame. It contains information about domestic flights from New York in 2013. Flights are departed from John F. Kennedy International (JFK), or Newark Liberty International (EWR), or LaGuardia (LGA) airports. The flights data set contains the following variables - * year - year of departure of the flight
* month - month of departure of the flight * day - day of the month of departure * dep_time - actual departure time * sched_dep_time - scheduled departure time * dep_delay - delay in departure in minutes * arr_time - actual arrival time * sched_arr_time - scheduled arrival time * arr_delay - delay in arrival in minutes * carrier - contains the name of the carrier * flight - flight number * tailnum - identification number on the tail of the flight * origin - origin city of the flight * dest - destination of the flight * air_time - total time spent in air * distance - distance between origin and destination airports * hour - represents hour * minute - represents minutes * time_hour - represents time and hour together

On inspection of the data, following observations are made - 1. The flights dataset has 336776 rows and 19 columns 2. The column names are - year ,month, day, dep_time, sched_dep_time dep_delay, arr_time, sched_arr_time arr_delay, carrier,flight, tailnum, origin, dest, air_time, distance, hour, minute,"time_hour 3. Departure time and departure delay time of 8255 cases are missing 4. Arrival time of 8713 cases are missing 5. Arrival delay of 9430 cases are missing 6. Air time of 9430 cases are missing 7. Mean departure delay is 12.64 mins (rounded to 2 decimal places) 8. Mean arrival delay is 6.9 mins (rounded to 2 decimal places)
